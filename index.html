5 ways to actually stick to a digital planner

  1. Pick one home screen. Pin your planner app to your tablet's dock so opening it takes zero friction.
  2. Plan the week on Sunday. Ten minutes on the weekly page beats scattered daily decisions.
  3. Track habits, don't judge them. An empty circle is data, not failure — look for the pattern, not perfection.
  4. Keep one notes page open for the random ideas that would otherwise break your focus.
  5. Review monthly, not just daily. The monthly overview is where you catch drift before it becomes a problem.
